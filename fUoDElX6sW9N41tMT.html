About Urban Farm Connect

"Empowering local farmers and urban retailers to create sustainable, community-driven food systems."

Urban Farm Connect was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize the way local farmers and urban retailers connect and do business. Our platform serves as a bridge between the agricultural heartland and the bustling city centers, ensuring that fresh, locally-sourced produce reaches urban consumers while supporting small-scale farmers.

Our Journey

2020

Urban Farm Connect is founded by a group of tech enthusiasts and agricultural experts.

2021

Launch of our beta platform, connecting 50 farmers with 20 urban retailers.

2022

Expansion to five major cities, with over 500 farmers and 200 retailers on board.

2023

Introduction of our mobile app and AI-driven crop prediction tools for farmers.

Our Impact

Since our inception, Urban Farm Connect has:

  • Facilitated over $10 million in transactions between farmers and urban retailers
  • Reduced food transportation distances by an average of 65%
  • Helped increase income for small-scale farmers by up to 40%
  • Provided urban consumers with access to fresher, more diverse local produce

We're committed to continuing our mission of creating a more sustainable and equitable food system. Join us in reimagining the future of urban food supply!
